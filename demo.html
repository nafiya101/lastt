DEOMALI MOUNTAIN

Deomali is a picturesque location situated in the Eastern Ghats, near Jeypore in the Koraput district of Odisha, India. Dominated by verdant landscapes, Deomali is known for its highest peak in Odisha, which is an attraction for trekkers and nature enthusiasts. The peak offers panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and forests.
This area is blessed with rich biodiversity and is often enveloped in a blanket of mist, providing a serene and mysterious ambience. The tribal culture of the local communities, such as the Kondh, Paraja, and Bhumia tribes, adds to the region's cultural richness, making it a fascinating place for those interested in anthropology and traditional Indian lifestyles. Visitors can experience the tribal way of life, their dances, music, and handicrafts. The drive to Deomali itself is a scenic journey through winding roads, lush greenery, and terraced fields. The tranquil environment, accompanied by the chirping of birds and a cool climate, makes Deomali a rejuvenating retreat away from the hustle and bustle of city life. .
